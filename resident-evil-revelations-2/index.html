Resident Evil Revelations 2

Good game. I don't particularly like the swapping characters thing when playing single player but it's not a deal breaker.

The controls and gameplay are slick, the story is servicable and the riot mode is fun. I also like the setting, stuck on an island where they are doing mad experiments with the T-Virus.

The episodic formula doesn't work well for me. I like the idea of having the secondary character not being a combat focused shooty character but in practice it's a bit boring playing as them in coop.
