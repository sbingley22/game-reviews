Resident Evil Revelations

Solid game.

Cheesy, what you'd expect story from a resident evil game that's serviceable enough.

The gameplay is good, movement and shooting feels pretty good even if the animations are a bit off. It's what you'd expect from the modern style third person RE games. Dodge system is a bit finicky.

The story has a decent length, not too short or long. There is quite a bit of replay value with raid mode and unlockable character / skins / weapons. I sank a good few hours into raid.
